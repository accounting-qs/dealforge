Apollo Suggestion Corpus Backs the title / industry / location chip-row typeahead

The lead-list chip rows read their autocomplete from a Supabase corpus instead of hitting Apollo on every keystroke. The corpus is refreshed by running ~75 curated seed queries (titles, industries, locations) against Apollo and replacing the stored values per type. Cold-miss queries (something the rep typed that isn't in the corpus yet) fall through to live Apollo and write the result back, so the corpus self-enriches automatically.

Cost: Apollo's search endpoints don't charge credits, so refresh whenever you like — there's no fixed cadence.
